Queen: The Greatest Band of All Time

Queen are a band that formed in London in 1970. They became very popular in the 1970s and 80s with hits like "Bohemian Rhapsody", "We Will Rock You", and "Another One Bites the Dust." The original members were Freddie Mercury, Brian May, Roger Taylor, and John Deacon.
